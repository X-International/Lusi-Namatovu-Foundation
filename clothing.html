The Need for Basic Support

Many elderly individuals in Rakai District struggle to access basic necessities such as adequate clothing, hygiene supplies, and bedding. Limited resources, mobility issues, and lack of family support can leave them vulnerable and unable to maintain their health and dignity.

The Lusi Namatovu Foundation's Clothing & Basic Needs program aims to provide these essential items, ensuring that elderly individuals are comfortable, healthy, and treated with respect. We believe that providing basic necessities is a fundamental part of our commitment to improving their overall well-being and quality of life.

How We Help

Our Activities

Supporting elderly individuals in Rakai District with essential items for comfort and dignity.

Clothing Distribution

We distribute new or gently used clothing appropriate for the local climate, ensuring elderly individuals have adequate protection from the elements. This includes:

  • Traditional and modern clothing options
  • Weather-appropriate attire
  • Footwear for improved mobility
  • Special occasion clothing for dignity

Hygiene Kit Provision

We provide hygiene kits containing essential items to promote cleanliness and prevent the spread of disease. Each kit includes:

  • Soap and body wash
  • Dental hygiene items
  • Personal care products
  • Sanitary supplies

Bedding and Blanket Distribution

We distribute blankets and bedding to provide warmth and comfort, especially during colder months. Our bedding packages include:

  • Warm, durable blankets
  • Bed sheets and pillowcases
  • Mattresses when needed
  • Mosquito nets for protection

Basic Needs Assessment

We conduct regular assessments to understand and address individual needs, including:

  • Personal care requirements
  • Mobility aid needs
  • Home safety assessments
  • Regular follow-up visits

Eligibility Criteria

Who We Help

Our program is designed to support those most in need in Rakai District.

Age Requirement

Must be 60 years or older, with priority given to those aged 70 and above.

Location

Must be a resident of Rakai District, Uganda, with verification from local community leaders.

Need Assessment

Demonstrate a clear need for clothing, hygiene supplies, or bedding due to financial constraints or lack of family support.

Living Situation

Priority is given to those living alone or with limited family support networks in the community.

Note: While these are our general criteria, we assess each case individually and may provide support in special circumstances. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
