Our Mission: Uplifting Rakai's Vulnerable

The Lusi Namatovu Foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of elderly individuals and empowering people with disabilities in Rakai District, Uganda. We provide essential care, support, and opportunities to ensure every individual can live with dignity, respect, and inclusion.

Our work includes delivering nutritious meals, medical care, and safe shelters for the elderly, while providing wheelchairs, crutches, and rehabilitation services for people with disabilities. We also advocate for their rights and promote social inclusion, fostering a stronger, more compassionate community.

Star icon representing aspiration and hope.

Our Vision: A Thriving Rakai for All

We envision a Rakai District where elderly individuals are valued, respected, and receive the care they need, and where people with disabilities are fully included in society with equal opportunities to thrive. Our dream is a community where everyone can live with dignity, free from stigma and barriers.

We strive to build a more inclusive and equitable society by breaking down discrimination, ensuring access to essential services, and fostering a culture of compassion and support. Our ultimate goal is a Rakai where every individual can live a fulfilling life, regardless of age or ability.

Mission vs. Vision: Understanding Our Purpose

Our mission defines what we do today—the actions we take to support Rakai's elderly and people with disabilities, like providing meals, shelters, and wheelchairs. Our vision describes our aspiration for the future—a Rakai where everyone thrives with dignity and inclusion.

Think of our mission as the roadmap we follow each day, and our vision as the destination we aim to reach: a more equitable and compassionate community for all.

Our Core Values: Guiding Our Work

The Lusi Namatovu Foundation is guided by principles that shape every decision and action we take to support Rakai's elderly and people with disabilities.
