Our Impact in Rakai

At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we empower Rakai's elders and people with disabilities through targeted programs that provide care, support, and opportunities for independence. From safe housing to assistive devices, our work ensures dignity and inclusion for all.

Since 2016, we've impacted over 170 lives—building homes, providing wheelchairs, and fostering community connection.

170+ Lives Impacted | 26+ Homes Built | 62+ Wheelchairs Provided

Our Program Goals

Our goal is to build safe homes for Rakai’s elders, ensuring they live with security and dignity.
