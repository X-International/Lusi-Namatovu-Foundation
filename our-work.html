Our Impact in Rakai

At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we empower Rakai's elders and people with disabilities through targeted programs that provide care, support, and opportunities for independence. From safe housing to assistive devices, our work ensures dignity and inclusion for all.

What We Do

Our Key Programs

Discover how we're making a difference in Rakai through our targeted support programs

Elder Housing

We build safe, accessible homes for elderly individuals in Rakai, ensuring they have shelter that meets their specific needs and promotes dignity in their later years.

50+ Homes Built
200+ Elders Sheltered

Mobility Aids

We provide wheelchairs, crutches, and other mobility devices to people with disabilities in Rakai, enabling independence and increased participation in community life.

100+ Wheelchairs
250+ Lives Changed

Skills Training

We offer vocational training in crafts, tailoring, and farming to people with disabilities, creating pathways to economic independence and sustainable livelihoods.

40+ Trainees
75% Now Earning

School Support

We provide school supplies, accessible transportation, and advocacy to help children with disabilities in Rakai attend school and receive quality education.

120+ Children Supported
90% School Attendance

Health Support

We facilitate medical checkups, medication access, and health education for elderly individuals and people with disabilities, improving overall wellbeing in Rakai.

300+ Medical Visits
85% Health Improvement

Community Outreach

We organize awareness campaigns and community events to reduce stigma around disability and aging, building a more inclusive and supportive environment in Rakai.

24 Communities Reached
5,000+ People Educated
