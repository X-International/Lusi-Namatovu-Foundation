Our Commitment in Action

Transforming Lives in Rakai District

At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we are deeply committed to transforming lives across Rakai District. Our targeted, compassionate programs empower vulnerable elderly individuals and people with disabilities by providing essential care—including nutritious meals, accessible healthcare, and vital emotional support. We actively foster independence with assistive devices and collaborate closely with local communities, training caregivers and developing infrastructure like safe homes, always striving to ensure dignity, inclusion, and a brighter future in every village we serve.

Since our 2016 inception, creating tangible, positive change for those facing unique challenges has been our driving force. We measure our success not just in immediate relief provided, but in the enhanced dignity, improved community well-being, and the lasting connections we build. By focusing on providing essential resources sustainably and strengthening local support networks, our programs aim to deliver enduring results that reflect our unwavering commitment to truly sustainable development within Rakai.

Our Focus

Uplifting Rakai's Elderly with Compassion

In Rakai District, many elders face isolation, declining health, and lack of consistent support. Our programs aim to directly address these challenges by providing safe homes, nutritious food, essential healthcare, and vital social connection, ensuring they can live their later years with the dignity and respect they deserve.

Safe Shelter Solutions

Construct secure, sturdy homes to ensure safety and comfort for Rakai’s elders.

Holistic Health Support

Provide accessible medical care and vital medications to enhance elders’ well-being.

Nutritious Meal Provision

Deliver daily nutritious meals to nourish and sustain Rakai’s elderly community.

Essential Daily Support

Supply warm clothing and daily essentials to meet Rakai’s elders’ basic needs with care.

Fostering Community Bonds

Create welcoming spaces to build strong community bonds and reduce elder isolation.

Our Mission

Empowering Rakai’s Disabled with Opportunity

At the Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering people with disabilities in Rakai District through transformative programs that provide mobility aids, accessible healthcare, skill-building opportunities, inclusive social spaces, and strong advocacy for their rights. Our work ensures they can live with dignity, independence, and a sense of belonging in their communities.

Our Program Goals

Targeting Key Needs in Rakai
