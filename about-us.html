Why We Exist

Our Pledge to Rakai

At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we are committed to uplifting the lives of elders and people with disabilities in Rakai District, a rural region where access to basic services is limited. Through tailored programs, we aim to build safe shelters for elderly individuals, distribute wheelchairs and crutches to people with disabilities, and support children with disabilities to attend school by providing school supplies, ensuring they can thrive with dignity, respect, and equal opportunities in the coming years.

Guided by compassion and community collaboration, we partner with local leaders, volunteers, and organizations in Rakai to create lasting, sustainable solutions. For example, we aim to build homes for the elderly using locally sourced materials, which lowers costs and supports the local economy, while ensuring our efforts address the unique challenges faced by vulnerable elders and people with disabilities in rural Rakai District.

Sheltering Our Elders

Providing safe homes and nutritious meals for seniors in Rakai, ensuring they live with dignity and comfort in their later years.

Empowering Through Mobility

Equipping people with disabilities in Rakai with wheelchairs, crutches, and other assistive devices to enable independent living.

Our Future in Rakai District

A Rakai Where All Belong

Our vision is to create a Rakai District where elderly individuals and people with disabilities live with dignity, independence, and full participation in community life.

"A community where everyone belongs, regardless of age or ability."

In Rakai, we envision a future where every elder has a safe home and access to compassionate care, living with dignity and respect in their later years. We see a community where people with disabilities move freely with appropriate mobility aids, participating fully in village life with equal opportunities to work, learn, and connect.

Our Path Forward

Key milestones in our journey to transform Rakai

Initial Assessment

Comprehensive needs evaluation and pilot projects in two Rakai communities

  • Complete community mapping of elder needs
  • Conduct disability accessibility assessment

The Impact of Our Vision

Our Guiding Beliefs

Our Values

Our values shape every step we take in Rakai, ensuring our work uplifts elders and people with disabilities with care and respect.

Compassion in Action

We meet every elder and person with a disability in Rakai with empathy, ensuring they feel seen and supported.

Respect for All

We honor the dignity of Rakai's elders and people with disabilities, valuing their voices and choices.

Inclusion for Everyone

We create a Rakai where all belong, building spaces for connection and equal participation in community life.

Empowering Lives

We equip Rakai's people with wheelchairs, crutches, and skills to live independently and thrive.

Integrity at Heart

We work in Rakai with honesty and transparency, ensuring every resource entrusted to us supports elders and people with disabilities responsibly.

Community Driven
