Why We Exist

Our Pledge to Rakai

At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we are committed to uplifting the lives of elders and people with disabilities in Rakai District, a rural region where access to basic services is limited. Through tailored programs, we aim to build over safe shelters for elderly individuals, distribute wheelchairs and crutches to people with disabilities, and support children with disabilities to attend school by providing school supplies, ensuring they can thrive with dignity, respect, and equal opportunities in the coming years.

Driven by compassion and community collaboration, we work hand-in-hand with local leaders, volunteers, and partners in Rakai to create sustainable solutions that last. For instance, we aim to build homes for the elderly using locally sourced materials, reducing costs and supporting the local economy, while our vocational training programs will teach over 40 individuals with disabilities skills like basket weaving and tailoring, enabling them to earn a living and contribute to their communities despite the unique challenges of rural Rakai District.

Sheltering Our Elders

Providing safe homes and nutritious meals for seniors in Rakai, ensuring they live with dignity and comfort in their later years.

Empowering Through Mobility

Equipping people with disabilities in Rakai with wheelchairs, crutches, and other assistive devices to enable independent living.

Our Future in Rakai District

A Rakai Where All Belong

In Rakai, we dream of a future where every elder has a safe home and access to care, living with dignity in their later years. We see a community where people with disabilities move freely with wheelchairs or crutches, joining in village life with equal opportunities to work, learn, and connect.

By 2028, we aim to make this real—building sturdy homes for elders, providing mobility aids for those with disabilities, and creating inclusive spaces for community gatherings. Together, we can ensure no one in Rakai is left behind.

"A community where everyone belongs."

Providing wheelchairs and crutches to empower people with disabilities.

Creating inclusive spaces for gatherings to strengthen Rakai’s community bonds.

Our Guiding Beliefs

Our Values

Our values shape every step we take in Rakai, ensuring our work uplifts elders and people with disabilities with care and respect.

Compassion in Action

We meet every elder and person with a disability in Rakai with empathy, ensuring they feel seen and supported.

Respect for All

We honor the dignity of Rakai’s elders and people with disabilities, valuing their voices and choices.

Inclusion for Everyone

We create a Rakai where all belong, building spaces for connection and equal participation in community life.

Empowering Lives

We equip Rakai’s people with wheelchairs, crutches, and skills to live independently and thrive.

Integrity at Heart

We work in Rakai with honesty and transparency, ensuring every resource entrusted to us supports elders and people with disabilities responsibly.

Community Driven

We partner with Rakai’s people to create solutions, honoring their knowledge and traditions in every program we build together.
