Our Commitment to Rakai's Elders

In Rakai District, many elders face challenges like limited healthcare access, poor housing, food insecurity, and social isolation, making daily life a struggle. The Lusi Namatovu Foundation is dedicated to changing this by providing care and support to help them live with dignity.

Our programs aim to meet their most urgent needs—offering safe homes, medical care, nutritious meals, and opportunities for connection—while fostering their well-being and inclusion in the community.
