Who We Are

Bringing Hope to Rakai Since 2016

Founded in 2016 by Samuel Ssebandeke, the Lusi Namatovu Foundation began with a heartfelt mission in Rakai District. After witnessing his elderly aunt, Christina Namazzi, living in neglect, Samuel was moved to act. Today, we provide food, shelter, medical care, and emotional support to Rakai's elderly, while also empowering the disabled community with essential resources, helping both groups live with dignity and hope.

Since then, our mission has grown to also include people with disabilities, who often face similar struggles of neglect, poverty, and isolation. We provide assistive devices, access to healthcare, and opportunities to live with independence and dignity. At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we believe that everyone regardless of age or ability deserves to feel valued, cared for, and included in their community.
