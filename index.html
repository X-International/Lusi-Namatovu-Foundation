Who We Are

Transforming Lives in
Rakai District Since 2016

Lusi Namatovu Foundation was founded in 2016 by Samuel Ssebandeke after returning to Uganda from the United Kingdom. During a visit to Rakai District, he found his elderly Aunt, Christina Namazzi, living in heartbreaking conditions, neglected and struggling without basic care. That moment opened his eyes to the silent suffering of many elderly people across Uganda who, after a lifetime of hard work, receive little to no support. Driven by compassion and a deep sense of responsibility, Samuel launched the foundation to provide food, shelter, medical care, and emotional support to elderly individuals in need.

Since then, our mission has grown to also include people with disabilities, who often face similar struggles of neglect, poverty, and isolation. We provide assistive devices, access to healthcare, and opportunities to live with independence and dignity. At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we believe that everyone regardless of age or ability deserves to feel valued, cared for, and included in their community.
