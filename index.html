Welcome To Lusi Namatovu Foundation

Hello, my name is Samuel Ssebandeke, Founder and Director of Lusi Namatovu Foundation, a non profit organisation fighting hunger and empowering the Elderly individuals in Rakai District. The less privileged elders need food, love and care. This is the initial reason why we support and provide for destitute old aged persons. Every month we provide rice, sugar among other basic needs to these individuals who are less privilaged or neglected by their families. Due to poverty some families are not able to feed these elderly persons. Some of them used to beg from their neighbouring houses to feed themselves. Our little contribution to the elderly makes their world healthy and happy

Our Concerns

In many African countries, an individual is not entitled to government assistance after retirement unless they have worked for the government. Why We Support the Neglected and Vulnerable Elderly Individuals

Challenge

Some of the elderly people in Rakai District are suffering from poor nutrition, lack of proper care and ill-health solution. The age group of these older people is from 65 and above. The health conditions of those destitute elderly people has been improved. Most of them are having anemic problem due to lack of food and medical care yet the goverment of Uganda has turned a blind eye. At Lusi Namatovu Foundation, we solve these problems by providing nutritious meals, clothing and medical care every month.

Solution and Long-term Impact

We provide Vegetarian Meals, Meat, Milk, Rice, Sugar, Salt among others. Such meals are easy to digest and healthy too. The health condition of destitute elderly persons has been improved. We offer them peer support so that they are free from worries.This will stop starvation among destitute elderly people in Rakai District. They get timely nutritious food and medical care. Eldery persons living conditions & life span will increase.The perception of the community on caring for the old aged people will build positively.
