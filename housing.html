Addressing Shelter Challenges in Rakai

Many elders in Rakai District live in unsafe housing, exposed to harsh weather, lacking basic amenities, and without proper security, which threatens their health and dignity.

Through our Shelter & Housing program, we aim to build safe, stable homes for vulnerable elders, ensuring they have a secure place to live with the dignity they deserve.

Our goal is to create lasting change by providing elders with environments where they feel protected, comfortable, and valued in their community.

Goals for Shelter & Housing in Rakai

  • Build or renovate 25 homes to provide elders with safe, stable living spaces.
  • Support 50 elder households with home repairs for lasting safety.
  • Supply 100 elders with essentials like bedding and furniture for comfort.
  • Educate 75 elders on home safety and sanitation for healthier lives.
  • Advocate for better housing policies to improve Rakai's elder communities.

How We Support Rakai's Elders with Housing

Building and Renovating Homes

We build new homes for elders living in unsafe structures and renovate existing ones to enhance safety and comfort.

  • Building durable walls and roofs for protection.
  • Installing secure doors and windows for safety.
  • Adding accessible latrines for hygiene.

Repairing and Maintaining Homes

We provide repairs and maintenance to ensure elders' homes remain safe and functional over time.

  • Fixing leaky roofs and broken windows.
  • Addressing pest infestations for health.
  • Improving sanitation for better living conditions.

Distributing Essential Items

We distribute household items to enhance elders' comfort and well-being in their homes.

  • Providing bedding and mattresses for rest.
  • Supplying cooking utensils for daily use.
  • Offering water storage containers for convenience.

Educating on Safety and Sanitation

We teach elders home safety and sanitation practices to prevent accidents and promote health.

  • Fire safety and fall prevention techniques.
  • Hygiene practices to reduce illness.
  • Proper waste disposal for cleanliness.

Who Qualifies for Housing Support in Rakai

  • Be over the age of 65 years and identify as an elder in the community.
  • Reside in Rakai District, Uganda, as a permanent resident.
  • Face housing challenges, such as unsafe living conditions, risk of homelessness, or disability-related housing needs.
  • Have limited family support or financial resources to address housing needs.

How to Access Shelter & Housing Support in Rakai

  1. 1. Contact our office directly at +256 705 864322 or lusinamatovufoundation185@gmail.com to discuss your housing needs.
  2. 2. Visit our office at Rakai District, Uganda, P.O. Box 185 Kyotera, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 4 PM.
  3. 3. Get referred by a community leader, healthcare worker, or social worker who can connect you with our team.
  4. 4. Join our outreach programs or attend community meetings in Rakai to apply for support.
